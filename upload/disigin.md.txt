Design a premium, modern About Us page for "Opportunities Platform" — a global student opportunity platform helping students discover scholarships, fellowships, internships, research programs, conferences, competitions, and international experiences.

The page should feel emotional, trustworthy, inspiring, and mission-driven.

Visual style should match the main website:

* Clean and modern
* Inspired by Linear, Apple, Stripe, and Notion
* Strong typography
* Large whitespace
* Elegant card layouts
* Premium animations
* No stock photos
* No founder-focused storytelling
* Focus on students and impact
